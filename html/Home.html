Spend a day learning to build robots with local school kids!

"After having our first Robojam last year, the children knew how exciting it would be so were very enthusiastic!  We are really grateful to have such fantastic volunteers supporting our children to apply their computing skills purposefully and with such enjoyment" - Belinda King, Headteacher - Kobi Nazrul Primary School, Tower Hamlets

What are the ingredients of a robojam?

Your Team

A team-building day for you and your team to use your existing skills (whatever they may be!), while making a real and valuable contribution to the local community.  No experience required!  Just a group of willing colleagues or like-minded parents prepared to give a day of their time. 

School Children

Schools local to where you live or work will welcome you for the day to work in small teams with their students to build robots!  You'll use your soft skills, your creative skills, your technology skills and most importantly, your fun skills!  And the best part is that some of those skills will rub off on the children, leaving a lasting impression of how to approach an amazing challenge!

...and of course, Robots!

These cute little cardboard fellows with rubber wheels and Raspberry Pi brains.  In your teams you'll design, build, write code, test and refine your robot. Then you'll compete in three rounds of robot carnage to be crowned winner of the day! 

We'll provide everything you need for your team to enjoy a traditional team-building day at a local school.  But the difference is that your day will involve having a great deal of fun and transferring many different skills to young children, while giving them an opportunity to improve their self confidence and problem solving abilities.

If your team would like to take part, we'll organise a rewarding and fun-filled day that we guarantee you'll remember as a team-building day with a difference.
